NEET Rates in Europe

This choropleth map provides a geographical representation of NEET (Not in Education, Employment, or Training) rates across Europe. Countries are colored based on their NEET rates, with darker colors representing higher rates. This visualization helps to quickly identify regional patterns and disparities in NEET rates across the continent.
