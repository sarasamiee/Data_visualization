NEET Rates and Youth Unemployment Rate

This interactive scatter plot visualizes the relationship between NEET (Not in Education, Employment, or Training) rates and Education Expenditure rates across various European countries from 2015 to 2022. Each point represents a country in a specific year, indicating its NEET rate and Education Expenditure rate. Tooltips provide detailed information about each data point on hover, including the year, country, sex, NEET rate, and education expenditure. The legend differentiates the years by color, enhancing the plot's readability and analysis. This chart helps explore the correlation between NEET rates and education expenditure over time, and trend lines for each year highlight overall trends and patterns in the data.
