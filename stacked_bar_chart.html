NEET Rates by Sex

This stacked bar chart shows the composition of NEET (Not in Education, Employment, or Training) rates by sex (male/female) for selected European countries. Each bar is divided into sections representing the NEET rates for males and females, providing a clear comparison of gender differences in NEET rates across countries.
